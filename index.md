## About Me

I am a second-year graduate student at the School of Mathematics, Shandong University, majoring in Operations Research and Cybernetics under the supervision of Professor [Jin Yan](https://faculty.sdu.edu.cn/yanjin/zh_CN/index.htm). I received my Bachelor's degree from North China University of Water Resources and Electric Power in 2024. My research focuses on extremal graph theory and digraph theory, with particular interest in Hamiltonicity, degree conditions, and subgraph existence problems.

## Research Interests

- **Extremal Graph Theory:** subgraph existence, Hamiltonicity, and extremal conditions
- **Digraph Theory:** oriented graphs, Hamilton cycles, structural and algorithmic questions
